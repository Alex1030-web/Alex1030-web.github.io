About LaneTech

Lane Tech College Prep is the largest high school in Chicago and is known as the “School of Champions” because of its long history of academic and athletic success. It was founded in 1908 and originally focused on technical and engineering education. Today, Lane offers one of the most diverse sets of programs in the city, including STEM, arts, and college‑level courses. The school is also famous for its iconic green and gold colors and its massive campus, which serves thousands of students every year.LaneTech is also the 3rd ranked best school in Illinois
